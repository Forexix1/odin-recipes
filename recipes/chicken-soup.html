Incredible Polish chicken soup (Rosół)

Description

Imagine a meat broth so good that you eat it on its own! This is rosół, the queen of Polish soups and one of the most iconic Polish dishes. It is rich and meaty, served with thin egg noodles, and so comforting! According to every Polish grandma, it is a cure for the cold.

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken (no breats)
  • Dried mushrooms (preference)
  • Dried spices:bay leaves, allspice berries, black peppercorns
  • Fresh herbs:parsley lovage
  • Soup vegetables:onion, parsley root, celeriac, carrots, leek
  • more chicken pieces + giblets

Steps how to make Polish chicken soup (Rosół)

  1. Cut up the chicken into invidual pieces.
  2. Clean the meat.
  3. Put meat in the pot.
  4. Add herbs and spices to the pot (they should be under the meat).
  5. Prepare vegetables, peel the carrots, celery root and celeriac, cut in half.
  6. Add the vegetables to the pot.
  7. Add Cold water to the pot (Aaproximately 4-5cm) of water above the meat and vegetables.

    8. After adding Cold water, all the flavor from the meat goes to the water. Leftover meat is tasteless.

  8. Bring the soup to a gentle boil over medium heat. When it comes to boil, reduce the heat and cook uncovered over minimum heat for 6 hours for perfect taste (minimum 3 hours).
  9. When the time is up, carefully remove any scum and foam that is on the surface of the soup, then strain the soup through a fine-mesh strainer.
  10. Season the soup with salt to taste.
  11. Place some pasta in bowls and pour in the soup.
  12. Season the soup with ground black pepper and Enjoy!

How to serve

  1. Angel hair pasta and ground black pepper (preference).
  2. Chopped parsley adds a pop of color.
